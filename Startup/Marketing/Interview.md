## Identify the threat
- Design Flaws
	- Insufficient Time
		- Experiment needs a lot of time to see effect
	- Short-term Effect
		- Effect doesn't affect the long time result
	- Representativeness
		- Ask the wrong people
		- ex: LGBTQ marriage -> ask unrelated people
	- Feasibility Problem
	- Unable to prove or implement
		- Cannot get hold of data
	- Willingness = Action
		- 萬人響應一人到場
